Maanen Faces is a Nantes-based rock band formed in 2021. The quartet explores a dark psych universe where alternative rock meets the heavy undertones of stoner rock and doom metal. Their sound is built on a solid foundation of effect-laden bass and powerful drumming, layered with atmospheric synthesizers and deeply textured guitars.

Drawing from the moody energy of Black Rebel Motorcycle Club and Sonic Youth, as well as the heavy echoes of Electric Wizard or Deftones, Maanen Faces seeks to capture the tension of a world in flux. The band strives to translate this universe into an immersive experience, inviting the audience into their singular atmospheric landscape.
