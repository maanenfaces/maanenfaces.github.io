Maanen Faces is a Nantes-based stoner/psych doom band formed in 2021. At the crossroads of psychedelia, stoner rock and doom metal, the quartet crafts a dense and dark universe, inspired as much by Electric Wizard, Sonic Youth and Kyuss as by modern acts such as Deftones, Idles or King Gizzard & The Lizard Wizard.
