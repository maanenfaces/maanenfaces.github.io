Maanen Faces is a Rock band from Nantes (France) formed in 2021.

Combining psychedelic, doom and stoner influences, the band delivers a powerful and dense rock. These Selenite emissaries lost on Earth explore the struggle for survival through their music. It's mystical but without god, it's tragic but still alive.

After a first EP in 2024, mixing doom and cinematic horror, the band returns in 2025 with a new, longer, more immersive opus, where psychedelia flirts with the apocalypse.
