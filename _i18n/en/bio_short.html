Maanen Faces is a rock band from Nantes, formed in 2021. Through their music, at the crossroads of psychedelia, stoner and doom, the band develops a dense and dark universe, inspired as much by Electric Wizard, Sonic Youth or Kyuss as by the more modern sounds of Deftones, Idles and King Gizzard & The Lizard Wizard.
